The Birla Institute of Technology & Science, Pilani (BITS Pilani) is a private deemed university in Pilani, Rajasthan, India.[11] It focuses primarily on higher education and research in engineering and sciences. BITS Pilani is one of the first six institutes in India to be granted Institute of Eminence status.[12] According to 2012 data, BITS Pilani has an acceptance rate of 1.47%, making it one of the most exclusive technical universities in the world.[13][14]
